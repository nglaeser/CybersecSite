About USC Cybersecurity

  1. Home
  2. About

We are a group of people interested in computer, network, and physical security. We enjoy penetration testing, cyber defense, capture the flag contests, and other challenges. Our students and mentors work together to share resources, practice defense and exploits, and watch amusing videos.

In Spring 2014 a small group of ACM students came together to compete in the Palmetto Cyber Defense Competition (PCDC). They teamed up with the CEC IT Network Manager, Ronni Wilkinson, for a crash course in fundamental hardening and security practices.

After lots of practice, which placed them second at the event, the team continued working together to bring in students, learn about security, and reach out to mentors in the community. Now, the USC Cybersecurity Club is an official on-campus organization open to students, faculty, and staff from all fields.
